What is Dutchy CORP?

Dutchy CORP (also known as DutchyCorp AutoFaucet) is a multi-earning cryptocurrency platform that combines faucets, PTC ads, shortlinks, offerwalls, and an auto faucet feature. It's one of the more complete faucet ecosystems available.

The site is known for its auto faucet feature, which allows you to earn cryptocurrency automatically without manually clicking every few minutes. You earn "Dutchy" coins which can be converted to various cryptocurrencies and withdrawn via FaucetPay.

Dutchy CORP has been operating reliably and has built a positive reputation in the faucet community for consistent payouts and regular site updates.

How Dutchy CORP Works

  1. Login with your FaucetPay account
  2. Earn Dutchy coins through various methods
  3. Convert Dutchy to cryptocurrency of your choice
  4. Withdraw instantly to FaucetPay

Earning Methods

  • Auto Faucet: Earn passively while the page is open
  • Manual Faucet: Claim every few minutes
  • PTC Ads: Watch ads for Dutchy coins
  • Shortlinks: Complete shortlinks for rewards
  • Offerwalls: Surveys and offers for higher earnings
  • Tasks: Various micro-tasks available
  • Achievements: Bonus rewards for milestones
  • Referrals: Earn from your referrals' activity
