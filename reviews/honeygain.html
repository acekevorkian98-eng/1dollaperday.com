What is Honeygain?

Honeygain is a legitimate passive income application that pays you for sharing your unused internet bandwidth. Install it, let it run in the background, and earn money while doing absolutely nothing. It's as close to "true" passive income as you can get online.

The concept is simple: your internet connection has bandwidth you're not using most of the time. Honeygain lets businesses use that idle bandwidth for web intelligence, price comparison, SEO monitoring, and other legitimate purposes. In return, you get paid.

Since launching in 2019, Honeygain has grown to over 10 million users worldwide and has paid out millions in earnings. With a 4.0 rating on Trustpilot, 4.2 on Google Play, and 4.5 on the App Store, it's one of the most established and trusted bandwidth sharing apps available.

How Honeygain Works

The Setup

  1. Download the app on your device (Windows, Mac, Linux, or Android)
  2. Create an account and sign in

The app silently shares a small portion of your unused internet bandwidth. It doesn't affect your browsing, streaming, or gaming - it only uses what you're not using anyway.
