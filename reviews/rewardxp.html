What is RewardXP?

RewardXP is a GPT (Get-Paid-To) site launched in 2019 that distinguishes itself through a modern, clean interface and transparent earning system. The site aggregates offers from multiple offerwalls and survey routers, letting you earn points redeemable for cash and gift cards.

What sets RewardXP apart is its focus on user experience. The interface is less cluttered than competitors like Swagbucks, and the site is particularly popular among the r/beermoney community for its reliability and reasonable payout rates.

The site offers worldwide access (though US users get the most offers) and supports both traditional payout methods like PayPal and cryptocurrency options.

How RewardXP Works

  1. Create an account with email or social login
  2. Complete your profile to qualify for more surveys
  3. Choose earning methods - offerwalls, surveys, videos
  4. Earn points - 1,000 points = $1
  5. Cash out at $5 minimum via PayPal, crypto, or gift cards

Earning Methods

  • Offerwalls: AdGate, Offertoro, AdGem, Ayet Studios, and more
  • Surveys: Multiple survey routers available
  • Video watching: Hideout.tv integration
  • Mobile offers: App downloads and game offers
  • Daily bonuses: Login streaks and achievements
  • Referrals: 10% of referrals' lifetime earnings
