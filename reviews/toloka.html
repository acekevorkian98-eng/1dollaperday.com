What is Toloka?

Toloka is a crowdsourcing platform owned by Yandex (the "Google of Russia") that pays people to complete micro-tasks used for training AI and machine learning systems. Tasks include image labeling, audio transcription, content moderation, and data validation.

What makes Toloka unique is its incredibly low $0.02 minimum payout - the lowest in the industry. You can literally cash out after completing a handful of tasks, making it zero-risk to try.

The platform is used by major tech companies to generate training data for AI systems. While individual tasks pay very little (fractions of a cent), they're quick and always available.

How Toloka Works

  1. Sign up and verify your account
  2. Complete training for task types you want to do
  3. Accept tasks from the available pool
  4. Submit work and maintain quality scores
  5. Cash out at just $0.02 minimum

Common Task Types

  • Image labeling: Tag objects in photos
  • Audio transcription: Transcribe short audio clips
  • Content moderation: Flag inappropriate content
  • Data validation: Verify information accuracy
  • Text classification: Categorize text snippets
  • Search relevance: Rate search result quality
