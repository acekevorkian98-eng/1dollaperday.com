What is Cointiply?

Cointiply is a cryptocurrency faucet and GPT (Get-Paid-To) platform that's been around since 2018. It offers multiple ways to earn cryptocurrency including a faucet, PTC ads, offerwalls, surveys, and games.

The site is well-designed with a clean, modern interface that's easy on the eyes. It's one of the better-looking faucet sites out there, which makes using it more pleasant than many competitors with cluttered, ad-heavy designs.

However, while Cointiply looks good and has plenty of earning options, actually reaching the payout threshold can be quite a grind. It's an okay site overall, but requires patience and consistent effort to see any real rewards.

How Cointiply Works

The Coin System

Cointiply uses its own coin system where 1000 coins equals approximately $0.10 USD (rates fluctuate). You accumulate coins through various activities and can withdraw once you hit the minimum threshold.

Earning Methods Available

  • Faucet: Claim free coins hourly. Basic but reliable way to accumulate.
  • Daily Wheel: Spin once per day for bonus coins - this is actually one of the nicer features.
  • PTC Ads: Watch advertisements for coins. Some ads have issues with timers not counting down properly.
  • Offerwalls: Complete offers from third-party providers for larger payouts.
  • Surveys: Answer surveys for coins (availability varies by location).
  • Games: Play mini-games for small coin rewards.
  • Interest: Earn 5% annual interest on balances over 35,000 coins.

The Grind is Real

With a 50,000 coin minimum for withdrawal (~$5), reaching payout through faucet claims alone takes significant time. The site is best used alongside offerwalls or as part of a multi-site strategy.

Withdrawal Thresholds

The minimum payout is 50,000 coins (approximately $5 USD). This is on the higher side for faucet sites, making it a grind to reach especially for casual users.

Threshold Strategy

Consider using the interest feature - once you have 35,000+ coins, you earn 5% annual interest. Some users prefer to let their balance grow rather than withdraw immediately.

Known Issues

Ad Timer Problems

Some PTC ads don't count down properly - the timer freezes or doesn't register completion. This can be frustrating as you watch an ad but don't receive credit. Refreshing or trying different ads sometimes helps, but it's an ongoing annoyance.

This timer issue seems to affect certain ad providers more than others. It's not constant, but happens frequently enough to be worth mentioning. The site itself pays reliably once you earn coins - it's just the ad viewing experience that can be buggy.

Tips for Maximizing Cointiply

  1. Never miss the daily wheel: It's quick and can give decent bonus coins.
  2. Focus on offerwalls: These pay much more than faucet claims if you want to reach payout faster.
  3. Skip broken ads: If an ad timer isn't working, move to the next one rather than wasting time.
  4. Use the interest feature: Let your balance grow above 35,000 coins to earn passive interest.
  5. Stack with other faucets: Don't rely on Cointiply alone - use it as part of a multi-site routine.

Final Verdict

Verdict: Decent - Worth a Try

Cointiply is an okay faucet site with a nice design and good variety of earning options. The daily wheel is a nice touch, and the interface is pleasant to use. However, the grind to reach payout is real, and the ad timer issues can be frustrating.

It's a legitimate site that pays, but probably not the best choice if you're looking for quick payouts. Use it as part of a broader earning strategy rather than your primary faucet.

Bottom line: A decent option if you don't mind the grind, but there are easier-paying alternatives out there.
