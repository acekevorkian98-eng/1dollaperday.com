What is GG2U?

GG2U (Get Got 2 U) is a GPT (Get-Paid-To) site that launched in 2018 with a simple promise: higher payouts than competitors. The site aggregates offers from multiple offerwalls and claims to pay users 80% of the revenue they receive - higher than industry standard.

While GG2U has a smaller user base and less polish than giants like Swagbucks, it has developed a loyal following among beermoney enthusiasts who've verified that many offers do pay more here than elsewhere.

The site offers surveys, mobile app offers, video watching, and other typical GPT activities. The interface is basic but functional, and the site has maintained a good reputation for payment reliability.

How GG2U Works

  1. Sign up with email or social login
  2. Browse offerwalls - multiple providers available
  3. Complete offers - app downloads, surveys, signups
  4. Earn points - 1 point = $0.01
  5. Cash out at $7 minimum via PayPal, BTC, or gift cards

Earning Methods

  • Offerwalls: AdGate, Offertoro, AdGem, and more - complete offers for points
  • Surveys: Multiple survey routers including Theorem Reach
  • Video watching: Watch videos for small amounts
  • Promo codes: Occasional codes for bonus points
  • Referrals: Earn 10% of your referrals' earnings

Realistic Earnings

GG2U is best for offer completion rather than surveys. The "high payout" advantage is most noticeable on mobile game offers.

Pro tip: Always compare offer payouts between GG2U, Freecash, and Swagbucks before starting. GG2U often (but not always) pays more.

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Compare before starting: Check if GG2U pays more than Freecash/Swagbucks for the specific offer.
  2. Focus on mobile game offers: These have the biggest payout differences between sites.

  4. Screenshot everything: If an offer doesn't credit, having proof helps with support tickets.
