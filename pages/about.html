About $1perday!

Our mission, methods, and commitment to honest reviews

Our Mission

The goal is simple: help you earn at least $1 per day through legitimate online micro-earning opportunities. No get-rich-quick schemes, no unrealistic promises - just honest information about what actually works.

I started this site after spending years in the "beermoney" space, testing countless platforms and watching too many people waste time on scams or sites that barely pay. The micro-earning world is full of hype and misinformation. I wanted to create a resource that cuts through the noise.

Who's Behind This Site?

I'm a solo operator who's been active in the micro-earning community since 2019. I've personally tested every platform reviewed on this site - not just signed up, but actually used them long enough to reach payout thresholds and verify they actually pay.

Why "$1 per day"? Because it's an achievable, realistic goal. Too many sites promise hundreds of dollars per month from surveys and faucets - that's misleading. But $1/day? That's 100% doable with the right combination of sites and a consistent daily routine.

How We Review Sites

Every review on this site follows a consistent process:

  1. Personal Testing: I sign up and use the platform myself for at least 2-4 weeks before writing a review
  2. Payout Verification: I only recommend sites where I've successfully withdrawn earnings to my own account
  3. Realistic Earnings: I track actual time spent vs. earnings to calculate true hourly rates
  4. Community Research: I cross-reference with Reddit, Trustpilot, and other community feedback
  5. Regular Updates: Reviews are updated when platforms change their rates, features, or reliability

Our Rating System

Sites are rated on a 1-10 scale based on: earning potential, payout reliability, user experience, and time-to-payout. A score of 7+ means "Recommended" - it's worth your time. Below 5 means "Not Recommended."

Affiliate Disclosure

Let's be transparent: this site uses referral links. When you sign up for a platform through my link, I may earn a small commission or bonus at no extra cost to you.

However, my reviews are not influenced by referral programs. I've given negative reviews to sites with generous referral programs because they weren't worth users' time. The goal is always to recommend what's genuinely useful - not what pays me the most.

Referral earnings help cover hosting costs and the time spent testing and writing reviews. I appreciate anyone who uses them, but I also provide direct (non-referral) links when available.

What This Site Is NOT

  • Not a get-rich-quick guide: Micro-earnings are supplemental income, not a replacement for a job
  • Not financial advice: I share what works for me, but your results will vary
  • Not affiliated with any platform: I don't work for any of the sites I review
  • Not a guarantee: Platforms can change their terms, rates, or shut down without notice
