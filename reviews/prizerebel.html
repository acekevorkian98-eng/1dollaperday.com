What is PrizeRebel?

In a world where GPT sites come and go faster than crypto projects, PrizeRebel has been quietly paying users since 2007. That's nearly two decades of reliability. While Swagbucks gets all the press and Freecash gets the hype, PrizeRebel has been steadily building one of the best loyalty reward systems in the industry.

PrizeRebel is a classic GPT (Get-Paid-To) site based in the United States. You earn points (100 points = $1) by completing surveys, offers, watching videos, and doing various tasks. What makes it special isn't any single feature - it's the combination of a very low $2 minimum payout, fast processing, and a loyalty level system that can boost your earnings by up to 30%.

Over $20 million has been paid out to members, and the site maintains an excellent reputation across Reddit, Trustpilot, and the beermoney community. It's not flashy, but it works - and in this industry, that counts for a lot.

How PrizeRebel Works

Earning Methods Available

  • Surveys: Multiple survey routers including Your Surveys, Theorem Reach, and Precision Sample. Pay varies from 25 to 500+ points per survey.
  • Offer Walls: Complete offers from AdGate, OfferToro, AdscendMedia, and more. Mobile game installs and app trials can pay 500-5000+ points.
  • Tasks: Small micro-tasks like data entry, categorization, and verification. Usually 5-50 points per task.
  • Videos: Watch videos for points. As always, the pay is low (1-3 points per video set), but it's passive.
  • Referrals: Earn 20% of your referrals' earnings for life. One of the better referral programs out there.
  • Promo Codes: Regular promotional codes shared on social media and via email.
  • Contests: Monthly contests with cash prizes for top earners.

The Loyalty Level System (This Is the Good Stuff)

Here's where PrizeRebel really shines. As you earn points, you progress through loyalty levels that permanently boost your earnings:

Why Diamond Level Is Huge

At Diamond level, you're earning 30% more than new users on the exact same activities. That survey worth 100 points? You get 130. That offer wall task? +30%. Over time, this compounds significantly. Reach Diamond once and you stay there forever.

Payment Options

  • Gift Cards: $2 minimum for some options. Amazon, Visa, iTunes, Steam, and dozens more.
  • PayPal: $5 minimum (500 points). Usually processed within 24 hours.
  • Bitcoin: $25 minimum. Processed via Coinbase.
  • Direct Bank Transfer: Available in some regions.

Best Earning Strategy

  1. Grind to Diamond first: The 30% bonus is massive. Focus on high-paying offers initially to reach 16,000 points.
  2. Use multiple survey routers: If you get disqualified on one, try another. Different routers have different inventory.
  3. Compare offer walls: The same offer often appears on multiple walls at different rates. Always check.
  4. Complete daily tasks: Small consistent earnings add up and maintain your activity level.

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Rush to Diamond level: Make reaching 16,000 lifetime points your first priority. That 30% bonus is worth the grind.
  2. Fill out all profile surveys: Better matching = fewer disqualifications = more completed surveys.
  3. Check offer walls daily: High-value offers rotate frequently. A 3,000 point game offer today might be gone tomorrow.
  4. Use the "Hot Offers" section: PrizeRebel highlights offers that are crediting well and paying quickly.
  5. Don't waste time on videos: The points-per-hour ratio is terrible. Focus on surveys and offers instead.
  6. Screenshot offer completions: If something doesn't credit, support can help if you have proof.
  7. Refer friends after reaching Diamond: The 20% referral bonus on top of your 30% loyalty bonus is extremely valuable.
  8. Look for promo codes: Follow PrizeRebel on social media. Free points add up.
