What is FreeBitcoin?

FreeBitcoin (freebitco.in) is the oldest Bitcoin faucet still in operation, having launched in 2013. In the early days of Bitcoin when BTC was worth $100, faucets gave away substantial amounts. While payouts are much smaller now, FreeBitcoin has remained reliable for over a decade.

The site offers hourly faucet claims, interest on your balance (up to 4.08% APY), a weekly lottery, and gambling features. It's become more of a Bitcoin savings/entertainment platform than a pure faucet, but the free claims remain the core feature.

With over 50 million registered users and billions of satoshis distributed, FreeBitcoin has proven its longevity in a space where most faucets disappear within months.

How FreeBitcoin Works

Earning Methods

  • Hourly Free Roll: Claim once per hour for random satoshi amounts (8-200,000 sats possible)
  • Interest: Earn up to 4.08% APY on your FreeBitcoin balance
  • Weekly Lottery: Free lottery tickets with each roll, prizes up to 2.5 BTC
  • Rewards Points: Earn RP for bonuses and boosters
  • Hi-Lo Game: Optional gambling (not recommended - house always wins)

Payment & Trust

FreeBitcoin has been paying users since 2013. Withdrawals go directly to your Bitcoin wallet (no micro-wallet needed). The 30,000 satoshi minimum takes time to reach, but payments are reliable once requested.

Withdrawal Info

  • Minimum: 30,000 satoshis (~$15 at current prices)
  • Method: Direct to Bitcoin wallet
  • Processing: Usually within 24 hours (auto on Sunday)
  • Fees: Network fee deducted

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Don't gamble: The Hi-Lo game is designed to take your satoshis. Just claim and hold.
  2. Let interest compound: Leave your balance to earn 4.08% APY.
  3. Use Reward Points wisely: Buy boosts that increase your hourly claims.
  4. Set hourly reminders: Maximize claims if you want to grind.
  5. Join the lottery: It's free with every roll - someone wins every week.

Gambling Warning

Warning: Hi-Lo Game

The Hi-Lo multiply game is a gambling feature with a house edge. Many users lose all their accumulated satoshis trying to "multiply" their balance. We strongly recommend avoiding the gambling features and just using the free faucet claims.
