What is Attapoll?

Attapoll is a mobile survey app that pays you to complete surveys on your smartphone. It's one of the simpler survey apps available - just open the app, take available surveys, and cash out when you hit the minimum.

The app was developed in the UK and has become popular for its clean interface, low minimum payout, and reliable payments. It's available on both iOS and Android with over 10 million downloads.

While the pay per survey is modest, Attapoll is perfect for killing time on your commute or while waiting in line. Surveys are typically short (5-15 minutes) and optimized for mobile.

How Attapoll Works

  1. Download the app (iOS or Android)
  2. Complete your profile for better survey matching
  3. Take surveys when notified of availability
  4. Earn money for each completed survey
  5. Cash out at $3 minimum via PayPal or crypto

Key Features

  • Push notifications: Get alerted when surveys are available
  • Quick surveys: Most take 5-15 minutes
  • Referral program: Earn from friends who join
  • Charity donations: Donate earnings to causes
