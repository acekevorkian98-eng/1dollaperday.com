How CoinPayU Works

The Coin System

Everything on CoinPayU revolves around their internal coin system. You earn coins from every activity, then convert those coins to your chosen cryptocurrency when you withdraw. The conversion rates are transparent and displayed in your dashboard.

This is actually one of the things CoinPayU does well - the system is straightforward. You always know exactly how many coins you have, what they're worth, and what you need to reach payout. No confusing point multipliers or hidden conversion rates like some competitors pull.

Earning Methods Breakdown

  • Browsing (PTC Ads): View ads for coins. Steady supply refreshes daily. Low per-ad reward but consistent.
  • Offers: Complete app installs, game trials, sign-ups. This is where the bigger payouts are - some offers pay 100+ coins.
  • Surveys: Partner surveys through multiple providers (Bitlabs, Theoremreach, etc.). Typically 70-175 coins for 2-25 minutes of work.
  • Tasks: Micro-tasks like social follows, website visits, sign-ups. Quick and easy, 5-120 coins each.
  • Faucet: Claim free coins periodically - essentially free money for clicking a button.
  • Referral Program: Earn a percentage of what your referrals earn. The affiliate dashboard tracks everything.

Level & Streak System

CoinPayU has added a leveling system where you earn XP from activities and level up for bonus rewards. There's also a 7-day streak challenge - earn at least 1,000 coins each day to build your streak and unlock extra bonuses. It's a nice touch that rewards consistent users.
