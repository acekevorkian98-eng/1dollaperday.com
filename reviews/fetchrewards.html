What is Fetch Rewards?

Fetch Rewards is a receipt scanning app that rewards you with points for every receipt you scan - regardless of where you shop or what you buy. Unlike Ibotta which requires purchasing specific products, Fetch gives you base points for any receipt.

The app has over 17 million active users and has paid out over $600 million in rewards. It was founded in 2013 and has become one of the most popular receipt apps in the US.

Fetch partners with brands who pay for consumer purchase data. You earn points, they get valuable market research - it's a fair trade that doesn't require you to change your shopping habits.

How Fetch Rewards Works

  1. Download the app (iOS or Android)
  2. Scan any receipt within 14 days of purchase
  3. Earn base points - 25 points minimum per receipt
  4. Earn bonus points - For purchasing partner brands
  5. Redeem points - 1,000 points = $1
  6. Get gift cards - From $3 (3,000 points) and up

Types of Receipts Accepted

  • Grocery stores: Any grocery receipt works
  • Gas stations: Fuel receipts earn points
  • Convenience stores: 7-Eleven, Wawa, etc.
  • Restaurants: Dine-in and takeout
  • Retail: Walmart, Target, CVS, and more
  • E-receipts: Connect your email for automatic scanning

Realistic Earnings

Fetch earnings depend heavily on how often you shop and whether you buy partner brands.

The Math

If you scan 30 receipts/month at 25 base points each, that's 750 points ($0.75). Partner brands can multiply this significantly - a single offer might give 500-2000 points. Most users earn $3-10/month passively.

Pros and Cons

Pros

Cons

  • Base points are quite low
  • US only
  • No PayPal cashout

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Scan every receipt: Even 25 points adds up over time
  2. Check offers before shopping: Plan purchases around bonus offers
  3. Connect email: E-receipts are scanned automatically
  4. Stack with Ibotta: Use both apps on the same receipt
  5. Watch for special promotions: Holiday periods often have higher bonuses
  6. Refer friends: 2,000 points per referral
