What is TopCashback?

TopCashback is a cashback portal that gives you money back on purchases at thousands of online retailers. Founded in the UK in 2005, it's now available in the US and Canada and is known for having some of the highest cashback rates in the industry.

The platform boasts "No Membership Fees - Ever" and promises to pass the highest percentage of commissions back to members. With over 4,500 retailers and $350+ million paid out, TopCashback is a legitimate alternative (or addition) to Rakuten.

The key differentiator? TopCashback frequently offers higher rates than competitors. They claim to match or beat any competing cashback rate, making them worth checking before every purchase.

How TopCashback Works

TopCashback works like other cashback portals - you click through their site before shopping, and they share the commission they earn with you.

How to Earn Cashback

  • Shop through the portal: Click on a retailer link, make your purchase, and cashback is tracked automatically
  • Browser extension: Install their extension to get notifications when cashback is available
  • In-store cashback: Link cards for automatic cashback at some physical stores
  • Bonus offers: Special promotions offer elevated rates or bonuses

Cashback Rates by Category

  • Fashion/Apparel: 2-15% cashback
  • Electronics: 1-5% cashback
  • Travel: 2-8% cashback
  • Food delivery: 1-4% cashback
  • Subscriptions: Often one-time bonuses of $5-20

Payment Proof & Reliability

TopCashback has paid out over $350 million to members since 2005. Payment reliability is excellent - once cashback becomes "payable" (usually 30-90 days after purchase), you can withdraw immediately.

Payout Options

  • PayPal: $10 minimum, processed in 1-3 days
  • ACH transfer: $10 minimum, 3-5 business days
  • Gift cards: Often with bonus value (e.g., $10 for $9.50)

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Install the browser extension: Never miss cashback - it notifies you when shopping at partner stores.
  2. Compare rates: Check TopCashback against Rakuten before each purchase - rates vary.
  3. Stack with credit card rewards: Cashback is in addition to your credit card points.
  4. Check for elevated rates: TopCashback frequently runs promotions with boosted percentages.
  5. Use gift cards for extra value: Converting to gift cards often gives 2-5% bonus.

Potential Issues

The main complaint with TopCashback is occasional tracking failures. If your cashback doesn't track, you'll need to file a claim with proof of purchase. This happens maybe 5-10% of the time.

The "pending" period can also be frustrating - some cashback takes 90+ days to become payable, though this is standard across all cashback portals.
