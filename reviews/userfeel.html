What is Userfeel?

Userfeel is a usability testing platform where you get paid to test websites and apps while providing feedback. Companies pay Userfeel to find real users who can test their products and provide honest opinions - and you can be one of those testers.

Similar to UserTesting, Userfeel pays $10 for tests that typically take 10-20 minutes. You'll navigate websites, complete tasks, and speak your thoughts out loud while screen recording software captures your experience.

Userfeel has been operating since 2010 and is a legitimate alternative to UserTesting, especially useful since UserTesting doesn't accept testers from all countries.

How Userfeel Works

  1. Sign up and create your tester profile
  2. Complete a qualification test to show your testing abilities
  3. Receive test invitations via email
  4. Complete tests - record yourself using websites/apps
  5. Get rated by clients on your feedback quality
  6. Receive $10 per approved test via PayPal/Payoneer

What Tests Involve

  • Screen recording: Your screen is captured as you test
  • Think-aloud: Speak your thoughts as you navigate
  • Task completion: Follow scenarios like "Find and purchase a blue shirt"
  • Written feedback: Answer questions about your experience
  • Rating: Provide overall usability ratings

Equipment Needed

  • Computer or smartphone (depending on test)
  • Microphone (for voice recording)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Quiet environment

Realistic Earnings

Userfeel test availability varies based on your demographics and tester rating.

Test Type Duration Payment
Standard website test 10-20 min $10
Mobile app test 10-20 min $10

Monthly estimate: $20-100/month depending on test availability and your rating. High-rated testers get more opportunities.

Effective hourly rate: $30-60/hour when tests are available (making it one of the highest-paying beermoney activities).

Cons

Tips for Success

  1. Speak clearly and constantly: Dead air leads to rejected tests
  2. Be authentic: Give real reactions, not what you think they want to hear
  3. Follow instructions exactly: Complete all tasks as specified
  4. Respond quickly: Test opportunities fill up fast
  5. Maintain high ratings: Better ratings = more test opportunities
  6. Test your equipment: Ensure microphone and screen capture work

Final Verdict

Verdict: Recommended

Userfeel is a legitimate website testing platform with excellent pay rates ($30-60/hour effective). The main limitation is test availability - you won't get tests daily. Best used alongside UserTesting and Respondent for a diversified testing income.
