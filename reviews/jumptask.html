JumpTask Review

Multi-task crypto earning platform with JMPT token rewards

Quick Facts

  • Website: JumpTask.io
  • Type: GPT / Microtask Platform
  • Founded: 2022 (Estonia)
  • Users: 15+ million registered
  • Payment: JMPT Token (MetaMask/WalletConnect)
  • Minimum Payout: None (gas fees apply)
  • Our Rating: 6/10 - Decent

Rating Overview

JumpTask offers a variety of ways to earn their JMPT cryptocurrency token, from surveys and games to bandwidth sharing through their Honeygain partnership. While the platform itself is legitimate with over $3 million paid out to users, there are some rough edges that hold it back from being a top recommendation.

The platform has a solid 4.7/5 rating on Trustpilot from thousands of reviews, and the no-minimum-payout policy is nice. However, the MetaMask requirement can be confusing for crypto newcomers, some sections of the site have dead links, and gas fees eat into smaller withdrawals.

How JumpTask Works

JumpTask is a GPT (Get-Paid-To) platform that rewards you in their native JMPT token for completing various microtasks. The process is simple:

  1. Sign up using Google, Facebook, Apple, or connect a crypto wallet
  2. Complete tasks from surveys, games, bandwidth sharing, and more
  3. Earn JMPT tokens that accumulate in your account
  4. Withdraw to MetaMask or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

The JMPT token is built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and can be traded, held, or converted to other cryptocurrencies. At the time of writing, JMPT trades around $1.07 per token.

Ways to Earn

JumpTask offers multiple earning methods:

  • Surveys - Answer questionnaires for JMPT rewards
  • Games - Play games and complete gaming challenges
  • Honeygain Integration - Share bandwidth passively (their most lucrative option)
  • Social Tasks - Engage on Twitter, Telegram, Discord
  • App Testing - Test products and applications
  • AI Training - Help train AI models with simple tasks
  • Video Watching - Watch ads and videos

Pro Tip

The Honeygain integration is where the real money is. If you're already using Honeygain, linking it through JumpTask gives you fee-free crypto payouts instead of waiting for PayPal thresholds.

Realistic Earnings

What can you actually expect to earn?

  • Casual users: $20-50/month with quick daily tasks
  • Active users: $50-100/month with consistent effort
  • Top earners: $100+/month combining multiple methods + referrals

The Honeygain bandwidth sharing alone can bring in $5-30/month passively, which makes JumpTask worthwhile even if you ignore the other tasks.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • No minimum payout threshold
  • Multiple earning methods in one place
  • Honeygain integration for passive income
  • 4.7/5 Trustpilot rating (5,500+ reviews)
  • Over $3 million paid out total
  • JMPT token is tradeable on exchanges

Cons

  • MetaMask/crypto wallet required for withdrawal
  • Gas fees on every transaction
  • Some dead links and broken sections on site
  • Confusing for crypto beginners
  • Staking program ended (January 2025)
  • JMPT token value can fluctuate

Payment Information

Currency JMPT Token (Binance Smart Chain)
Withdrawal Method MetaMask, WalletConnect-compatible wallets
Minimum Payout None (but gas fees apply)
Processing Time Instant to wallet
Fees BSC gas fees (varies, usually small)

Important Note

You'll need to set up MetaMask or another BSC-compatible wallet to withdraw. If you're new to crypto, this can be a learning curve. Make sure to add the Binance Smart Chain network to your wallet and have some BNB for gas fees.

JumpTask + Honeygain

One of JumpTask's biggest advantages is its integration with Honeygain. If you're using Honeygain for passive bandwidth sharing income, connecting it through JumpTask means:

  • No payout threshold - Unlike Honeygain's $20 minimum
  • Crypto payments - Get paid in JMPT instead of waiting for PayPal
  • Bonus earnings - Sometimes offers +10% bonus for JumpTask payouts

Check out our Honeygain review for more details on the bandwidth sharing side.

Final Verdict

6/10 - Decent

JumpTask is a legitimate platform that pays out reliably, but it's not without issues. The dead links and broken sections are annoying, and the MetaMask requirement adds friction for casual users. However, if you're already comfortable with crypto wallets, the no-minimum-payout policy and Honeygain integration make it worthwhile.

Best for: Crypto-savvy users who want to consolidate earnings from multiple sources, especially Honeygain users looking to avoid the $20 PayPal threshold.

Skip if: You're new to cryptocurrency and don't want to deal with wallets and gas fees.
