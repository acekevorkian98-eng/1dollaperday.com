What is ySense?

If you've been in the beermoney game for a while, you might remember ClixSense - one of the original PTC (paid-to-click) sites that started back in 2007. In 2019, ClixSense rebranded to ySense and shifted focus from PTC to GPT activities, particularly surveys and offers. It was a smart move - the PTC model was dying, and ySense adapted.

Today, ySense is one of the most reliable GPT platforms for users worldwide. While US/UK users have more options and better rates (as always), ySense actually has decent survey availability for users in countries that are often ignored by other platforms. If you're outside the "Tier 1" countries, ySense might be one of your better options.

The site has a clean interface, reasonable payment minimums, and most importantly - a 15+ year track record of actually paying people. That kind of longevity is rare in this industry and says something about their legitimacy.

How ySense Works

Earning Methods Available

  • Surveys: The main earning method. ySense offers surveys from multiple providers including their own "ySense Surveys" as well as third-party routers. Pay ranges from $0.25 to $5+ depending on length and your profile.
  • Offer Walls: Complete offers from AdGate, OfferToro, AdscendMedia, and others. Mobile game installs and app trials can pay well.
  • Tasks: Micro-tasks powered by Figure Eight (now Appen). Data categorization, image tagging, and similar work. Pay is low but tasks are easy.
  • Daily Bonus: Earn activity bonuses by completing enough surveys/offers each day. Adds up over time.
  • Referrals: Earn up to 30% of your referrals' survey earnings. One of the more generous referral programs.

The ySense Add-On (Browser Extension)

ySense has a browser extension that notifies you when new surveys are available. More importantly, it allows you to:

  • Get instant notifications for matching surveys
  • Access surveys faster (they fill up quickly)
  • Track your daily progress

Global Availability Is the Selling Point

While US/UK/CA/AU users have the most surveys, ySense actually works in 100+ countries. Users in India, Philippines, Brazil, and other non-Tier 1 countries often find more opportunities here than on US-focused platforms. If other GPT sites have let you down due to location, ySense is worth trying.

Payment Options

  • PayPal: $5 minimum. Processing usually within 24-48 hours.
  • Payoneer: $5 minimum. Good option for countries with limited PayPal access.
  • Gift Cards: Various options including Amazon, starting at $5.
  • Skrill: $10 minimum. Alternative e-wallet option.

Survey Disqualifications Are Real

Like all survey sites, ySense has a significant disqualification rate. You might start 5 surveys and only complete 2. This is normal across the industry and is factored into the time estimates above. Don't let it discourage you - it's just how survey sites work.

Best Earning Strategy

  1. Complete your profile thoroughly: Better matching = fewer disqualifications.
  2. Install the browser extension: Catch surveys before they fill up.
  3. Focus on ySense Surveys: They tend to pay better and have better completion rates than third-party routers.
  4. Don't ignore offer walls: A single game offer can equal hours of survey work.
  5. Hit daily activity bonuses: Small but consistent extra earnings.

ySense (formerly ClixSense) is one of the oldest GPT sites still operating. They've adapted to industry changes, survived the death of PTC, and continue to pay users reliably. When a company has been paying people for 15+ years, that's a pretty strong endorsement.

The beermoney community generally views ySense positively. While it may not be the highest-paying option for US users (Freecash and Swagbucks often win there), it's considered one of the more reliable options for international users.

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

  1. Fill out ALL profile surveys: ySense uses these to match you with surveys. Incomplete profiles = fewer opportunities.
  2. Be consistent with profile answers: Survey providers check for consistency. Contradicting yourself gets you disqualified or banned.
  3. Install the ySense Add-On: Survey slots fill up fast. The extension gives you a heads up.
  4. Check multiple times daily: New surveys appear throughout the day. Checking once isn't enough.
  5. Don't ignore offer walls: While surveys are the focus, a single good offer can earn more than an hour of surveys.
  6. Aim for daily bonuses: The activity checklist bonuses aren't huge, but they add up.
  7. Try third-party routers when ySense Surveys are empty: They're often lower quality, but some opportunities are better than none.
  8. Cash out regularly: Don't let large balances sit. Cash out when you hit $10-20.

Final Verdict

Verdict: Highly Recommended (Especially for International Users)

ySense is a reliable, veteran GPT platform that does exactly what it promises. It's not the flashiest or highest-paying option for US users, but its 15+ year track record, global availability, and Payoneer support make it an excellent choice for international earners. The $5 minimum and fast processing are nice bonuses.

Best for: International users who struggle to find opportunities on US-focused platforms. Also good for anyone who values reliability and longevity over maximum earnings. If you're outside the US/UK and need a dependable GPT site, ySense should be on your list.
