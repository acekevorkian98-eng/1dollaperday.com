What is Surveytime?

Surveytime is a survey platform with a simple premise: complete a survey, get $1 instantly sent to your PayPal. No points to track, no minimum thresholds to reach, no waiting for payment processing.

The site is operated by Prime Opinion, a legitimate market research company that also runs RevenueWall. It's gained popularity in the beermoney community for its straightforward approach and instant payments.

While $1 per survey is lower than what you might get from Prolific or Respondent, the instant payment and zero minimum threshold make it attractive for users who want immediate cash.

How Surveytime Works

  1. Sign up with email
  2. Complete profile questions for better survey matching
  3. Take available surveys - typically 10-20 minutes
  4. Get $1 instantly sent to PayPal after each survey

The Surveytime Model

  • No points system: Just straightforward cash
  • Instant payment: PayPal arrives within minutes
  • Flat rate: Every completed survey pays $1
  • Pre-screening: You're matched to relevant surveys

The Key Benefit

Unlike most survey sites that require $5-20 minimum before cashing out, Surveytime pays you $1 immediately after completing each survey. You could complete one survey and cash out the same day.
