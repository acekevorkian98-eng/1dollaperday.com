What is Qmee?

Qmee is unique among survey sites because it has absolutely no minimum payout. You could complete one survey for $0.30 and cash out immediately to PayPal. No waiting to accumulate $5 or $10 - your earnings are instantly accessible.

Founded in 2012, Qmee started as a browser extension that paid you for clicking search results. Today it's evolved into a full survey platform while keeping the search rewards feature.

The no-minimum policy makes Qmee perfect for trying out before committing time, or for users who want instant gratification from their survey efforts.

How Qmee Works

  1. Sign up for a free account
  2. Complete surveys matched to your profile
  3. Install browser extension for search rewards (optional)
  4. Cash out any amount instantly to PayPal

Earning Methods

  • Surveys: Main earning method, various providers
  • Search rewards: Browser extension shows paid links in search results
  • Cashback: Shopping cashback at partner stores
  • Games: Play games for small rewards
  • Referrals: Bonus for friend signups

The Search Rewards Feature

Qmee's browser extension shows sponsored results alongside your normal Google/Bing searches. When you click these results, you earn a few cents.

How It Works

Search normally on Google, Bing, Yahoo, Amazon, or eBay. Qmee shows relevant sponsored links in a sidebar. Click if interested and earn $0.03-0.15 per click. Not intrusive - you can ignore them if not relevant.
